The Data Open; A Citadel Datathon at University of Waterloo

Last updated on May 24, 2018 1 min read

Data Open is a competition/hackathon that brings together the best minds in mathematics, engineering, STEM to collaborate and compete using the world’s most important data sets. Over 100 undergraduate and graduate students from University of Toronto, Waterloo are invited to participate after an initial 60 minute assesment test. Our team won the first place for our work Improving 311 service through Geospatial and Time Series Analysis
